What is Ecommlipa?

Ecommlipa is our flagship fintech solution: an API-embedded Buy Now, Pay Later platform for e-commerce checkouts. Our asset-light B2B2C model ensures no credit risk to us—banks handle approvals with Y1 limits from KES 1,000-100,000, increasing as we scale. We operate on a 1.5% take rate from our 30% share of bank fees.

The Ecommlipa Story

The idea for Ecommlipa was born when Paul Mucheru wanted to buy a KES 30,000 cabinet from a supermarket but had no cash. He was told to pay KES 10,000 upfront and finish paying the full amount after 3 months to take the cabinet. He also witnessed relatives taking smartphones on 'lipa mdogo mdogo' options with exorbitant interest rates.

Having worked in the financial sector, Mucheru envisioned a solution that would connect merchants with credible financial institutions, allowing customers to take goods on loan and enjoy them while paying back to trusted institutions at fair rates.

When his employer didn't pursue the idea, Mucheru partnered with experts Alphonse Omesa, Emmanuel Ngetich, Samuel Ngila, Kevin Owino, and Boniface Cherotwo. Together, they built a successful prototype in 2023 that demonstrates complete end-to-end functionality, and partnered with FasterCapital to secure investment for scaling across East Africa.
